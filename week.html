The Non-Negotiables
Chelsea hears from you before she asks
Friday brief, every week, without being chased. If something risks missing Chelsea's expectation, she hears about it from you — not from a number. Problems arrive to her as situations you already have a plan for.
Every deal has a next action
Any deal in the pipeline without a concrete next action and a date is not a pipeline deal — it's an aspiration. Kill it or give it a 48-hour deadline. Every Monday you know the real number.
TLs coach, not report
A TL who only tells you dial counts is managing activity. A TL who tells you what specific line a rep used at minute 3 and what they coached them to say instead is coaching. The difference is the whole programme.
Recognition is specific or it doesn't land
"Great week everyone" does nothing. "When Sarah said 'what does your Tuesday look like at 2pm?' before mentioning TGTG — that's exactly why she closed it" — that's the recognition that teaches the whole team.
The standup sets the day's energy
A rep who leaves the standup knowing exactly what they're going to do differently on their next call has a better day than one who leaves with a number to hit. Energy is the output. The number is the result.
Close on the call. Live same day.
Every rep knows the standard: close the Surprise Bag window in the same call, walk the partner through the app, first bag live before hanging up or same day. A verbal yes that isn't live is not a close.
