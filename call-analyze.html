How to get a transcript from Salesforce
If using Salesforce Einstein Conversation Insights: Open the activity record → click the call → find the transcript tab → select all and copy.

If using a connected dialler (e.g. Aircall, Dialpad, Gong): Open the call log in Salesforce → click through to the recording → find the transcript or auto-generated notes → copy the full text.

If no auto-transcript: Play the recording and type key moments — even a partial transcript (opening, objection handling, close attempt) gives the AI enough to score the critical criteria.

Quick tip: The rep name and call date usually appear at the top of the Salesforce activity record. Include them in the paste so the coaching output is named correctly.
