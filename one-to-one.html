Listening principle: Note the exact words used at each stage. Paraphrase is not enough for coaching — the rep needs to hear what they actually said, not a summary. The most powerful coaching moment is playing back the specific line that landed or the specific moment that broke the flow.
01 Call Stages — Observed in Sequence
Opening (first 30 seconds) Did they establish who they are and why this call is worth 90 seconds?
Discovery question Did they ask about the venue's quiet period before pitching?
TGTG pitch Did they frame TGTG as a solution to the problem the owner just named?
Objections raised and handled
Close or next step Did they ask for a meeting / visit / decision?
02 Call Skills Scorecard
Opening and framing
Confident opener — clear who, why, and the hook
Asked one discovery question before pitching
Listened — paused after questions, didn't fill silence
Pitch and close
TGTG value prop clear — not just "we drive bookings"
Personalised to what the owner said
Asked for the meeting / next step / close
