The Mission — know this before every call
The pitch is not about the platform. It is about turning a zero into revenue. Every rep needs to believe this before they dial.
The core truth: Every unsold Surprise Bag is food that already exists, already cost money to make or buy, and is heading for the bin. Without Too Good To Go, that food is worth £0 — or worse, a disposal cost. With Too Good To Go, it becomes revenue. We are not asking a business to discount their product. We are asking them to recover value from inventory they were going to lose.

One in three meals produced globally goes to waste. Too Good To Go has saved over 350 million meals worldwide. Every partner a rep adds to this platform is a direct, permanent reduction in that number.
How it works — say this on every call
Simple enough to explain in 30 seconds. Powerful enough to close on the same call.
"You create a Surprise Bag of whatever you have left at the end of the day — food that would otherwise go to waste. You set the price, the collection window, and the number of bags. Customers on our app see it, reserve it, pay for it in advance, and collect directly from you. You get the money. They get a great deal on quality food. The food doesn't go to waste.

It costs nothing to join. No contract. No minimum. You can pause any time. The only cost is the percentage we take from each bag sold — and that bag was going in the bin anyway, so every penny is revenue you didn't have."
